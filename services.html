Order Meal is one of NZ's fastest-growing providers of online ordering systems for restaurants. With customers ranging from neighbourhood takeaways to fine-dining restaurants, we are confident in our ability to provide an online ordering solution that will work for you. We've worked with hundreds of restaurants across the country over the last five years and have formed some great partnerships along the way!

When you work with Order Meal, you can rest easy knowing you're working with a team that will help your establishment succeed online. We'll help you design your online ordering website, build and implement your online ordering system, make it part of our amazing mobile app, and we can even give your customers the ability to order in-store securely using our dedicated hardware if needed. Order Meal also offers free support and training for all orders placed through our system.

  • Intuitive Design and Layout
  • Tons of Custom Features

  • Secure Payment System

Since 2016 it has been our mission to equip restaurant owners with a web-based platform that allows them to have their own online ordering system so they can increase sales, minimise waiting time for customers, and take bookings at the touch of a button. We are confident all these features will offer you an unprecedented service to attract new customers, not just on your website but also on-site.

We are sure you'll be impressed by the services we offer, but we are more than happy to help outside of our usual scope, in any way possible. Our customer service team is extremely dedicated and responsive, full of people who aim to make every step clear for our clients.

It’s quick and easy to get started with the solution that is right for you. Simply speak to one of our friendly team members over the phone or book a meeting with us and we'll even come to your location. Tell us a little about your business, your requirements, and within just a few days, we can usually have an online ordering system up and running. We would love the opportunity to help you grow your sales with our unique services tailored to restaurants like yours so please don't hesitate to contact us now.
