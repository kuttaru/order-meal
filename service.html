Reserve a Table

“Reserve a table” is an exclusive feature that both customers and restaurants can use whenever they want.

If all tables are full, restaurants can add their guests to the waiting list. What’s more, they can confirm or cancel bookings, track how much time guests spent on a table, and they can even pull reports on bookings. Moreover, this feature can be integrated into the customer website at any point.

Valuable insights to optimise your restaurant operations

The Reserve a Table feature is beneficial because you will be in total control of the entire reservation process. It also allows you to access valuable information that can help you run your business more efficiently.

You can keep track of how long guests are staying at their table so that you know how much time guests spend at your restaurant. Plus, you also get to pull reports on bookings, which can help you improve the way you work and allows you to optimise your staff or manpower.

Restaurants can also use this feature to confirm or cancel bookings. For example, if there is a sudden change in the schedule you can cancel bookings up to X hours before they start. This way, you can ensure that tables are free or wait times will be reduced.

This feature not only provides extra comfort and convenience for those who reserve a table but also allows you to reach potential customers by offering a premium service.

If you own or manage a restaurant and you wish to use this feature, contact the Order Meal team now.

Providing customers with a premium and convenient experience

Customers don’t need to stand outside just to get in. They can now reserve tables through our app or via your website. Besides that, customers receive notifications when their reserved table becomes available (timely notice). Customers also get automatic confirmations and reminders about their reservations.

When tables are full, they can add themselves to a waiting list. Moreover, they can also cancel or modify their reservations whenever necessary. This feature not only saves customers’ time but also reduces costs. The end result is that both restaurants and customers are pleased.

With Order Meal, customers never worry about not having a table at their favourite restaurant. They can avoid the embarrassment of waiting for a table when they're with friends and they get seated faster than ever!

It’s so easy to reserve a table at your favourite restaurant with Order Meal!

If you're a restaurant owner or manager, be sure to contact the Order Meal team to learn more about how you can use the Reserve a Table feature to improve your business operations!
