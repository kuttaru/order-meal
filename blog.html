The Four Best Seafood
dishes that you can eat today

Seafood is extremely popular during the summer, but you can consume it at any point in time. That’s the real beauty of it. With so many different seafood dishes, though, which ones are the tastiest to eat? This is a very subjective thing and the answers will vary from one individual to the next. Still, some options are clearly better than others, and in the lines below, we will cover the four best seafood dishes that you can eat in 2020. All of these are perfect choices and you won’t make a mistake with any of them.

Let’s get started!

Goan Fish Curry

One thing is certain - you can never really go wrong with curries! And when we talk about fish curries, in particular, you just know that you are in for a real treat!

What are you getting with Goan Fish Curry? Well, you are getting a delicious fish fillet cooked in a fine thick tomato and coconut gravy with some cream and lemon as a bonus. This is an ideal meal for dinner, although, you can eat it for breakfast too. And if you have a nice salad, it can be a perfect lunch as well.

If all this sounds appealing to you, visit Yellow Mirchi and see what they have to offer first-hand!

Prawn Malabri

Do you like Butter Prawn? What about Prawns Masala? If you like at least one out of these two iconic dishes, you will definitely like Prawn Malabari as well. Moreover, if you appreciate traditional Malabari foods, this may easily be your favorite prawn overall.

The recipe itself is very basic. All we need are regular prawns cooked in a thick curry sauce and in a true Malabari fashion. The strength of Prawn Malabari doesn’t lie in its complexity but flavor. It’s hard to describe what the taste feels like, it’s the type of magic that you need to experience on your own. The sooner you do it, the better.

You can get a top-notch Prawn Malabari at The Red Fort.

Fish Madras

The most “original” entry on this list comes in the form of Fish Madras. No matter how much you like seafood in general, this dish will blow your mind. Literally. Once you take the first bite, it’s very hard to stop.

So, what are you getting with Fish Madras? You’re getting a traditional South Indian curry specially prepared with coconut and a whole variety of different spices. The curry itself is already incredibly delicious, but this combination of different aromas is what will certainly make you come back for more.

Want to try Fish Madras as early as today? Virsa Restaurant is waiting for your order!
